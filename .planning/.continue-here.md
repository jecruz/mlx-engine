## Hard Constraints

- Do not remove the restore-time `mx.eval(...)` safety barrier as a shortcut.
  Earlier no-restore-eval evidence failed warm restore with a cross-thread MLX
  stream error.
- Do not retry full-prefix KV span packing without a different
  write-amplification plan. It regressed persistent-cache warm restore
  performance.
- Do not treat benchmark process exit `0` as success without inspecting
  row-level errors.
- Do not treat a copied model directory or LM Studio model-data entry as
  sufficient for live validation. `lms ls --json` must expose the retained VLM
  model key first.
- Do not run live LM Studio `/v1/chat/completions` validation for retained
  LFM2.5-VL until
  `scripts/lmstudio_vlm_live_validation_preflight.py` reports
  `ready_for_live_validation=true`.
